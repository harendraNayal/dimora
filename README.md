Privacy Policy – Dimora Night

Dimora does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

The app uses the “Display over other apps” permission solely to dim the screen and provide night mode and OLED red mode functionality across apps.

Dimora does not access user files, contacts, location, camera, microphone, or network data.

No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used.
